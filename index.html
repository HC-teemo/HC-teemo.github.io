Toyota Research Institute

Toyota Research Institute is Toyota's Bay Area research arm, advancing autonomous driving technology through on-road testing and demonstrations. From 2017 to 2019, I partnered with their UX team to:

  • Build demos to showcase research and point of view on autonomy
  • Build debug interfaces for Field Operations to test vehicles on the road
  • Conduct user studies around safety, trust, and situation awareness
  • Solve UX challenges re: vehicle intervention and driver agency
  • Imagine the future of multi-modal, AI-powered driving
  • Architect principles of behavior for in-car AI
  • Unify TRI's internal design language

PayPal

PayPal has been making online payments safe and easy since the early aughts.


In 2019, I helped their team refresh their design system guidelines from the ground up. We audited design patterns across the org, re-architected the pages, revised the guidance, and created visual aids.


In 4 months, we brought more clarify their design language and increased team velocity by 4x.

SoulCycle

SoulCycle is a high-energy, boutique spinning class with a cult following.


In 2015 and 2016, I designed for their digital experiences — the iOS booking app and website.


SoulCycle for iOS created an enormous lift in class bookings. It helped riders get out of the app and onto their favorite bike.

Phosphor for Android

For Android users who enjoy theming their phone, Toby Fried and I created a family of line-style icon packs to achieve a simple, friendly look. Each icon is crafted with minimal lines and geometric shapes to be clear, reductive, and quirky.


Our first release went live in 2019. Today, we provide 870 icons (and counting!) in 4 colors.

Prolific Interactive

In 2015, I spearheaded a brand refresh to better reflect the spirit of Prolific Interactive, a mobile-focused product agency. Like many agencies, the internal brand came second to client projects and it was an uphill endeavor, but we did it!


The rebrand took us through core values, brand identity, tone of voice, internal documents, and culminated in a new website to showcase our team and our work.
