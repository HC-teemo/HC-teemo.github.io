Toyota Research Institute

Toyota Research Institute is Toyota's Bay Area research arm, advancing autonomous driving technology through on-road testing and demonstrations. From 2017 to 2019, I partnered with their UX team to:

  • Build demos to showcase reasearchg and point of view on autonomy
  • Build debug interfaces for Field Operations to test vehicles on the road
  • Conduct user studies around safety, trust, and situation awareness
  • Architect principles of behaviour for in-car AI
  • Imagine what the future of driving could look like
  • Unify their internal design language

It was a joy to prototype multi-modal experiences (screens, light, sound), tackle UX challenges around vehicle intervention and driver agency, and xx.

PayPal

PayPal is the OG online payments platform. In 2019, I helped their design system team rebuild their guidelines from the ground up, re-architecting, rewriting, and creating visual aids for 29 pages, covering the majority of PayPal's design language.


In 4 months, we shipped the refreshed guidelines, increasing team velocity by 4x and infusing a more meticulous approach to understanding, desgning, and documenting components.

SoulCycle

SoulCycle is one of the original boutique fitness studios in metropolitan areas, offering high-energy indoor cycling classes. In 2015 and 2016, I designed for their iOS booking app and a website refresh in fall 2015.


SoulCycle for iOS [revolutionized] the way riders reserved classes and created an enormous lift in bookings. It got them out of the app — and onto their favorite bike.

Phosphor for Android

For some Android users, theming their home screen is a form of self-expression and a way to keep the experience fresh. In 2019, Toby Fried and I shipped a family of icon packs to achieve a simple, friendly look.


Phosphor for Android includes 870 icons, available in 4 colors. Each icon is crafted with minimal lines and geometric shapes to be clear, reductive, and quirky.

Prolific Interactive

In mid-2015, I spearheaded a brand refresh to better reflect the spirit and energy of Prolific Interactive, a mobile-focused digital product agency. Like many agencies, the internal brand came second to client projects.


We codified core values, defined the brand identity, and developed our tone of voice. We relaunched our website, putting people first with portraits throughout the site and showcasing our work through simple language, powerful imagery, and dynamic rhythm of the page. We refreshed internal documents and tools.
